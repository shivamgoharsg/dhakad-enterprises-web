Our Services

we are provide in this areas

Water Purifiers:

Dhakad Enterprises provides cutting-edge water purifiers designed for homes. These purifiers utilize advanced filtration technologies to ensure that the water you drink is free from impurities and contaminants.

Commercial RO Systems:

Commercial RO Systems: For businesses, industries, and institutions, Dhakad Enterprises offers robust commercial reverse osmosis (RO) systems. These systems are tailored to handle larger volumes of water and are crucial for maintaining water quality in commercial settings.

RO Plants:

The company specializes in setting up RO plants for communities, factories, and large-scale applications. These plants are essential for ensuring a sustainable and clean water supply.

Water Softeners:

Dhakad Enterprises also provides water softeners that help in reducing the hardness of water. Soft water not only enhances the life of appliances but also ensures a more comfortable bathing and washing experience.
